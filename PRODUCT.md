# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Active retail forex and CFD traders who journal trades during or after market sessions. They open the dashboard at 8am before the London open, scan performance in under 10 seconds, and move back to their charts. They are not reading — they are scanning. Power users log 5–20 trades per week and review patterns weekly.

## Product Purpose

FXEDGE is a professional trading journal that enforces discipline through structured logging, visualizes performance through equity curves and pair analytics, and surfaces patterns through AI coaching. Success means a trader who logs consistently, spots their edge faster, and stops repeating the same mistakes.

## Brand Personality

Precise, fast, trusted. Like the tool a serious trader would build for themselves if they could code.

## Anti-references

- Neon-on-black crypto dashboards (garish, performative)
- Generic SaaS bootstrap admin panels (indistinct, soulless)
- Bloomberg terminal clutter (overwhelming, no hierarchy)
- Anything with gradient text or glassmorphism as decoration

## Design Principles

1. **Scan before read.** Every screen should communicate its most important number in under 2 seconds without reading a single word.
2. **Density earns trust.** A trader trusts a tool that respects their time. Generous padding signals inexperience. Compact, well-spaced data signals craft.
3. **The tool disappears into the trade.** UI chrome should recede. Data is the foreground. Components, cards, and dividers are background infrastructure.
4. **State is information.** Green is profit, red is loss, amber is caution. Color carries meaning — never use it decoratively.
5. **Redundancy wastes cognitive load.** Every label, heading, and sub-label must earn its place. If it repeats information already shown, remove it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Color meaning always paired with shape or text (never color alone). Reduced motion respected.
